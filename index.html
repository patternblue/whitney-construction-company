About Us

"Third Generation of Professional Service"

Whitney Construction is a family owned business servicing the Greater Pittsburgh Area and surrounding counties since 1945. We specialize in Commercial & Residential Construction. Our services run from small projects such as home repairs to large projects such as construction of commercial buildings.


Our Mission is to provide quality service at a reasonable price. We understand that choosing a contractor to come into your home or business is an important decision. When you choose Whitney Construction, you are choosin a qualifed contractor that will respect your time and needs. We will work with you from beginning to end to assure your 100% satisfaction.
